Weekend Trip Ideas

4 routes
Alsace Weekend
Strasbourg — Christkindelsmärik
Capital of Christmas since 1570. 300+ chalets across 12 locations, Great Tree at Place Kléber.
Kaysersberg — Artisan Market
Albert Schweitzer's birthplace. Intimate weekend market beneath castle ruins. Bredele & vin chaud.
Colmar — Marchés de Noël
Six markets in a picture-perfect old town. "Petite Venise" canals lit up. Runs to Dec 29.
~1.5h to Strasbourg 2 days ideal Nov – Dec
Cologne + Aachen Day Trip
Aachen — Aachener Weihnachtsmarkt
100+ stalls around the UNESCO cathedral. Famous Aachener Printen cookies. Living nativity.
Cologne — 7 Christmas Markets
Cathedral market with 150 stalls beneath the Dom. Warm Kölsch beer. 7 distinct themed markets.
~2h to Aachen 1 long day or overnight Nov – Dec 23
Pfalz Local Crawl
Kaiserslautern — Stiftsplatz Market
Your home-base market. Pfälzer Glühwein, Bratwurst, handmade crafts.
Bad Dürkheim — Wine Road Market
Spa-town charm. Exceptional Riesling Glühwein from local estates. ~30min from KL.
Neustadt — Wine Road Capital
Half-timbered Marktplatz setting. Heart of the Deutsche Weinstraße. ~35min from KL.
Speyer — Cathedral Market (to Jan 6!)
UNESCO cathedral along Maximilianstraße. One of few markets running through New Year's.
All under 45min 1 day or split weekends Nov – Jan 6
Belgium Weekend
Liège — Village de Noël
200 chalets. Closest Belgian market (~2.5h). Authentic Liège waffles & boulets.
Brussels — Plaisirs d'Hiver
Grand-Place light show. 200+ stalls, ice rink, Ferris wheel. Waffles, chocolate, beer.
Ghent — Gentse Winterfeesten
150+ chalets along medieval waterfront. Less touristy than Bruges. Runs to Jan 3.
Bruges — Wintergloed
Medieval canals + Winter Glow light trail. Most romantic market in Belgium. To Jan 3.
~3h to Brussels 2–3 days Late Nov – Jan 3
